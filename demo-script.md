## Demo Script

### Scenario 1: Booking an Appointment
**Customer:** Hi, do you have any appointments available this week?

**Luna:** Hi! Thanks for reaching out to Glow Studio. I'd be happy to help you book an appointment. What service are you looking for? We do haircuts, colouring, styling, and more.

**Customer:** I'd like a haircut please.

**Luna:** Great choice! We have appointments available:
- Tuesday at 2pm
- Wednesday at 10am or 3pm
- Thursday at 11am

Which works best for you?

**Customer:** Wednesday at 3pm please.

**Luna:** Perfect! I've booked you for a haircut on Wednesday at 3pm. Can I get your name and phone number?

**Customer:** It's Sarah, 07700 900123

**Luna:** Wonderful! I've booked:
- **Service:** Haircut
- **Date:** Wednesday
- **Time:** 3pm
- **Name:** Sarah

You'll receive a text confirmation shortly. See you then!

---

### Scenario 2: Pricing Question
**Customer:** How much is a haircut?

**Luna:** Our haircuts start at £35 for a basic cut and styling. If you'd like a more complex style or consultation, it's £50. Would you like to book an appointment?

---

### Scenario 3: After Hours
**Customer:** Hi, are you open tomorrow?

**Luna:** Thanks for reaching out! Our hours are:
- Monday: Closed
- Tuesday-Friday: 9am-6pm
- Saturday: 9am-4pm
- Sunday: Closed

It's currently after hours, but I've logged your inquiry. Would you like me to book an appointment for when we reopen?

---

### Scenario 4: Cancellation
**Customer:** I need to cancel my appointment.

**Luna:** No problem! I can help with that. Can you confirm your name and the appointment time?

**Customer:** It's Sarah, Wednesday at 3pm.

**Luna:** I've cancelled your haircut appointment for Wednesday at 3pm. If you'd like to rebook, just let us know. Thanks!
