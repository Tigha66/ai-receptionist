# AI Receptionist Business Roadmap

## The Big Picture

**Target:**
- $30K MRR = 75 clients @ $399/mo
- $1M ARR = 170 clients @ $499/mo

---

## Phase 1: Foundation (Month 0-1)
**Goal: 10 Clients ($4K MRR)**

- [ ] Pick ONE vertical (Plumbing, HVAC, Dental, etc.)
- [ ] Build vertical-specific offer
- [ ] 40 cold calls/day
- [ ] 30 cold emails/day
- [ ] Offer 14-day trial
- [ ] Close 2-3 per week

---

## Phase 2: Prove & Optimize (Month 2-3)
**Goal: 25 Clients ($10K MRR)**

- [ ] Refine onboarding
- [ ] Improve scripts
- [ ] Track conversion metrics
- [ ] Start small paid ads
- [ ] Partner with 2-3 local agencies
- [ ] Target: +7-10 clients/month

---

## Phase 3: Systemize (Month 4-6)
**Goal: 75 Clients ($30K MRR)**

- [ ] Hire commission-based sales rep
- [ ] Hire appointment setter (VA)
- [ ] Stop prospecting manually
- [ ] Add second vertical (HVAC or electricians)
- [ ] Target: 15 new clients/month

---

## Key Metrics to Track

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| MRR | $30K |
| CAC | < 1.5 months revenue |
| LTV:CAC | > 3:1 |
| Churn | < 4% monthly |
| ARPU | $399-499 |

## Success Formula

1. **Niche down** - Own one vertical first
2. **Prove ROI** - Track and share numbers
3. **Systematize** - Hire and automate
4. **Scale** - Add verticals and partners
